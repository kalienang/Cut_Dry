My name is Sheri Becker and I’ve been a Cosmetologist for almost 30 years. I started my career in a large busy salon that employed 20+ stylists. I worked there for 7 years and received a great deal of experience from co-workers as well as Style Directors that came into the salon for continued education. I then became independent by renting a chair which enabled me to become more flexible with a growing family. This took me where I am today. I own my business and operate out of my home, which gives me flexibility with my clients as well as my family. I’ve continued with education seminars periodically to stay current and up-to-date with styles and techniques. My hours are convenient and prices are affordable. The conversation is good and I hope to see you soon!
